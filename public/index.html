Origami

Origami is a hobby I got into for over a year and it is so much more than a 12 step crane. For the basics, most people need to know the difference between a:

    Mountain Fold - A fold made away from you.
    Valley Fold - A fold made toward you.

Origami requires a few requirements to make more of the professional models such as large square-shaped paper that is thin. Everyone has their own preference on origami paper and certain kinds of paper are better on certain models than others. A model that I have infamously been unable to make is Satoshi Kamiya's Crane, which requires the thinnest kind of paper known as tissue paper

Every model has their different decree, some artists create simpler models for cuteness or elegance, while others have models that are hundreds of steps to accurately portray the fine details of a certain species of animal. There are so many origami artists in the world and they are have their own style of folding; for example origami artists from China are coined for the pleat-fold, which are a series of folds creating the ressemblance of scales.
